About Me

Hi, I’m Yeswanth — a marketing analytics and measurement strategist passionate about helping brands understand what truly drives growth.

My work sits at the intersection of Marketing Mix Modeling (MMM), experimentation, and incrementality testing, where I translate complex analytical outputs into simple, actionable guidance for marketing, media, and finance teams.

Based in NYC/NJ, I enjoy blending technical problem-solving with clear storytelling to influence budgets, shape channel strategy, and support smarter, more confident planning.
